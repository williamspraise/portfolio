LumiQuote -

The redesign - a challenge with

Home

/ LumiQuote

Re-shape and design a new Hi-Fi version of the Sourcing Scenario Page


Tool used -

I revamped the enterprise product experience - S.C.A.M.P.E.Ring it's existing platform and implementing new features and updates

LumiQuote, intending to help EMS (electronics manufacturing services) deliver faster and better services to their customers (OEMs). One of the biggest challenges for EMS is to navigate the big amount of offers for every needed part to find the best price and availability for their scenario

Overview -

Adopting the 3Ws (Who, What, Why), I drafted out a problem statement for my user persona as follows:

Problem Statement -

"Stella Corp. an EMS, wants to seamlessly navigate a large number of offers for every needed part requested by an OEM, to find the best price and availability for each RfQ received"

This problem statement was useful to validate the existing platform expereince. This statement would be the scope of my project.

Hypothesis Statement -

This has a solution statement of how I went about solving the problem statement, which of course is yet to be tested in the usability test.

"By implementing the Sourcing Model, we believe EMS providers like Stella Corp can after defining the part needed to be bought for the project, add a sourcing scenario which by choosing the order size and their preference, provide possible offers for users and best possible solutions. We expect to see EMS respond by sending out Quotations"

Business Goals -

1. To achieve a clearer and more pleasant UI

Goals -

1. To understand the system of operation

2. To proffer solutions to the user feedback.

Objectives -

1. Understand and define the problem by analyzing the Problem statement, Hypothesis Statement and Workflow.

2. Prepare a metric of success.

3. Ideate on means to achieve this success

4. Implement the design solutions

5. Prepare a usability study script and KPI

6. Remarks and Discussion.

Success Metrics -

1. 80% of EMS providers should seamlessly be able to parse visuals from aggregated BOM.

2. 85% of users identify urgency in each element from a first glance.

3. 90% of Stakeholders' goals should be met.

4. 75% of users should be able to under the visualization tab seamlessly.


UX frustrations -

• Difficulty to visually parse information from aggregated BOM tables to the Sourcing Scenario Page.

• The visualization tab has not been designed properly.

• Difficulty in Identifying which Item needs immediate attention


Urgent item is defined by : Cases when no offers could be found for parts as well as when the price or lead time are extremely high

Ideation  - How Might We

An ideation session was carried out to brainstorms on ways to improve the experience of the platform that solves most of the identified UX errors.
