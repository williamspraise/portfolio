/ The Bartender

Technology is fast growing and we need to grow with it —  Interaction Design is the game changer for product sale

Tool used -

I implemented the Interaction Design from one artboard (frame) of a Hi-fi mock up without duplicates.

Specializing in Interaction design, my job description was to sell a consumer product to its users adopting the lastest trend of storytelling to offer a suitable user experience.

Overview -

Hypothesis Statement -

It's already understood the stakeholders has seen a problem and are tackling solving it. Here has its hypothesis statement:

"By adopting the storytelling design menthods, we believe more users would seamlessly understand the product we intend selling, purchase products and staying connected — We expect to see customers understand our product offerings, benefits of purchasing our stock and as well adopt the platform for continous purchases"

Goals -

1. Translate Hi-Fi into functional Prototype

2. Have business in mind while making design decision

Objectives -

1. Perform wireframe skecthes

2. Create Storytelling narratives
