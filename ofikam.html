Home

/ Ofikam

Eventually we decide we want to relocate —  plan a vacation, rent or buy new apartments or process scholarships — Ofikam is developed to serve as an agency hub globaly.

Tool used -

I translated the concept sketches provided by the stakeholders in well thought UX process and represented it into visuals - UI

Being the Lead UX Designer in the project, my JD was to align the user needs, frustrations and prioritization with the stakeholders visions, goal and mission. ensuring the UX is critically thought of and translated into visuals which the users, interface with to acheiving their overall goals.

Overview -

Hypothesis Statement -

It's already understood the stakeholders has seen a problem and are tackling solving it. Here has its hypothesis statement:

"By developing an agency hub platform, we believe more users would seamlessly find, plan or contract agency of desired needs — We expect to see users get a new apartment, plan vacations and as well get travel visa from the domain of this platform"

Goals -

1. Translate concepts to well functional prototype

2. Assist Developers with Design Assests

Objectives -

1. Perform wireframe skecthes

2. Implement new screens and reveiw them with Teams
