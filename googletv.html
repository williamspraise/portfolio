/ GoogleTV

Analyse, adapt and study the art of storytelling

Tool used -

I implemneted the interaction design, taking into account the art of storyteling IxD.

Google TV offers a new product in the entertainment industry, the question of how to showcase this physical product offering digitally to a new audience was the goal. Adapting the storytelling interaction design must have been the right call. We will see how in the following sections.

Overview -

Opportunity Statement -

"This task is aimed at strengthening my ability to be able to prototype at least a 100%" interactivity which will produce usable data after a usability study before handing off to the development phase.

At the end of this task, we will validate if we accomplished that

Goals -

1. Identify how Interaction design - storytelling appraoch benefits a B2C

2. Implement at least 75% of the interactive prototype.

UX error -

• Google Logo : From the original google website with the hero page in focus, the logo at the Nav fades away to avoid redundancy of logos - as we have the logo as well at the body of the hero page, and appears as you drift away from the hero page.


Disadvantage: If users don't scroll up to let the nav bar appear on the screen, users will face the challenges of knowing identifying what platform they are in.

Modification

From the identified challenges above, I modified the logo to follow the same but in addition to it become visible after existing the hero page - This gives users the visual clue of their whereabout while interacting with any section of the landing page.
