Quickteller -

The redesign - a challenge with

Home

/ Quickteller

Today — Quickteller is arguably one of the most popular bill payment platforms used by many for several types of payment in Nigeria.

Tool used -

I designed a whole new approach to Quickteller - that delivers customers service and seamless navigation - and instant transaction when transacting.

1. Re-conceptualize / redesign the landing page and a selected Billers page by providing an improved User Interface and a functional user experience.


2. While you identify the elements to be redesigned, state briefly your reason, not choices why these elements require a redesign including your proposed new UX.


3. The design should be RWD.

Overview -

Persona -

Adopting the 3Ws (Who, What, Why), I drafted out a problem statement for my user persona as follows:

Problem Statement -

"Richie, a cashless spender wants to make transactions but can’t find the right intermediary payment services to aid a seamless, instant transaction between himself and the business vendors of his delight, leaving him unsatisfied"

"Richie — is a well to do financially stable bachelor enjoying his relationship with his fiancée. He loves the dynamic evolution of the cashless — society and has cultivated that lifestyle, as he loves shopping, and settling all bills before they terminate"


This problem statement was useful to validate the existing platform in regards to instant and seamless transactions. This statement would be the scope of my project.

Hypothesis Statement -

This has a solution statement of how I went about solving Richies problem from the problem statement, which of course is yet to be tested in the usability test.

"By reconceptualizing and redesigning the Quickteller website, we believe more users like Richie could optimize their use of Quicteller seamlessly with less thought to system operations and to make payment successfully — We expect to see users like Richie perform more transactions and leave a 4-5 star rating"

Business Goals -

1. We use a technology-first approach to payments and finance.

2. Secure and Instant Payment.

Goals -

1. To redesign the interface for the Quickteller landing page across the website and mobile.

2. To make sure users find these platforms usable and seamless

Objectives -

1. Define the problem by curating the Persona, Problem statement, Hypothesis Statement and Journey Map.

2. Prepare a metric of success.

3. Ideate on means to offering a great user experience

4. Implement the design solutions

5. Prepare a usability study script and KPI

6. Remarks and Discussion.

Target Audience -

1. eCommerce

Success Metrics -

1. 80% of the stakeholder’s goals should be met

2. 89% of users should find complementary platforms easy to navigate

3. 0.5% of users could experience drop off.

4. 70% of users choose to trade with this platform.


Existing Journey Map -

I created a journey map from the existing platform to identify the frustrations users like Richie goes through when using the platform.


Here is a look at the customer journey:

Link to Journey Map

Link to Journey Map

UX errors -

• Search bar is redundant — From a business perspective, the landing page ought to provide more assurance to its customer and also market itself alongside its services. A better put to use of the landing page could be structured to offer a better CTA for the page.


• Instant Customer services — It is important that B2B2C Business services should offer an instant Customer service to ensure they aid users achieve their goal and improve great experience.

No similarities with Interswitch - A group of users would navigate Quickteller from Interswitch, only getting there and not seeing similarities could leave most users lost which breaks our primary rule of don’t make the user think.


• Overall user journey — From Richie's user journey map we could find more reasons why reconceptualizing the existing journey map would serve as a great user experience.


• Sign In/Login — Due to the human factor of achieving their goals at the shortest time with less input with forms, we could take a look into integrating google sign in API and validation process to secure its account.

Ideation  - How Might We

An ideation session was carried out to brainstorms on ways to improve the experience of the platform that solves most of the identified UX errors.
